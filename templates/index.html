What is Tomato Vision..?

In agriculture, maintaining the health of tomato plants is crucial for ensuring food security and economic stability. However, diseases and pests often threaten tomato crops, leading to significant yield losses. To address this, modern technologies like deep learning and convolutional neural networks offer promising solutions. This project focuses on developing a CNN-based model for detecting diseases in tomato plants. By analyzing annotated images of healthy and diseased plants, the model learns to recognize signs of common diseases such as blight,spot,virus and mold.The goal is to provide farmers with a reliable tool for early disease detection and management. By integrating this model into agricultural practices, farmers can make informed decisions and improve crop productivity. Trained on a diverse dataset consisting of approximately 5000 images, Tomato Vision has been meticulously crafted to recognize subtle signs of disease or distress in tomato plants. With an outstanding testing accuracy of 94%, this model stands as a beacon of reliability in the landscape.
